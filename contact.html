When Imagination Becomes Reality

IDEA

Each project begins with an idea. With us you can make your Dreams come true. Tell us about your Idea and we will turn it into a real work of art, by creating a sketch of your vision on a piece of paper.

3D Modeling

The Second Important Step is 3D Modeling Process. After making a sketch of your model, it is time to transform the sketch into 3D Dimensional pattern. After sharing the render with you and getting your final agreement we can go to the third stage of the production.

3D Printing

In the third stage, the most important role is playing technology. After creating the 3D model of your Imagination; it is time to let the technology do its job. In the third stage we are printing the wax model from your 3D model by bringing the Visual 3D Dimensional model into a Real Wax pattern, which will become your unique piece of art in the future.

Casting

After Printing and already having the Wax model we should cast it into Gold or other Precise metals using special skills and Professionalism for the highest accuracy and safe molding. The wax material which we are using allows creating the perfect patterns of ready gold models.

Final Proceeding

After casting Process it is time for the Masters to make the latest final Procedures which will bring the Final view of your fantasy model. In the final stage of the Process we are putting stones, making corrections, polishing and preparing the Model to give for its customer.

Ready Model

After all Procedures You can Finally have your Ready model Which will give you a Positive mood and Real sense to feel the original Model of your imagination in your hands.
