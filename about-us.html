ABout us

WELCOME TO 3D Jewelry Design studio

3D Jewelry Design studio is the first opened company in Kaunas which is professionally engaged in jewelry making industry by using 3D technology and Computer aided design in its Production.

“We are here to help Jewelers to create unique models of masterpieces and enjoy the creative process of making jewelry. We offer our guidance to avoid unnecessary steps and finally create that first prototype or finished product”

If you have an Idea or you have some photo and want to have your own exclusive jewelry Model, Please feel free registering for the jewelry consultation, our jeweler with the Long years experience will help you to create the Real Model of your Imagination. Before having the Final Model we share photos and other information with our Client’s to be sure that the Final product which you will get will give you a positive mood and real sense of satisfaction ….

We are located in Kaunas and work with clients from all over the Europe.

WHY CHOOSE US

The 3D Jewelry Studio is a team of talented and creative jewelers and designers with extensive knowledge and over 25 years of experience in the jewelry making industry. We’re cooperating with our client’s giving to each of them Individual approach and working closely together in creating and developing the most unique and Original Models of your Fantasy. Whether it is designing fashion jewelry, fine jewelry or a concept jewelry collection!

We design every kind of jewelry and accessories: from engagement rings, wedding rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and Relief Designs, from the simplest models to any intricate and complex jewelry designs.

We are making Fine Jewelry for the Private clients as well.
